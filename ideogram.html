Ideograms

Simple Pseudo-Random Stuff

The Ideogram one is for drilling basic ideograms. Might be a bit of a stretch to call this a 'game', really. But if you are in the "ideograms should be drilled" camp, fill your boots.

The prompts are biological-organic, manmade, water, land, space-air, motion-energy, and natural. Once the Start button is pressed, one pseudo-random choice comes up every two and a half seconds.

Because it's just a pseudo-randomized deck of digital cards, you could also use this for practicing microPK and precognition.

Now I've figured out a nice, easy way to code this -- and knowing that not everyone who approaches ideograms this way uses the same bunch -- I'll probably do other sets.
